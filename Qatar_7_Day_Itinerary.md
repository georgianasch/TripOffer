# **7-Day Qatar Itinerary – Slow Pace**

**Best time to visit:** Spring (March–April) or Autumn (October–November) – comfortable temperatures 20–28°C.

---

## **Day 1 – Arrival in Doha**
- Check-in and settle at your hotel.
- Take a **stroll along the Corniche** – enjoy views of the modern skyline.
- Dinner at **Souq Waqif**: try local dishes like *machboos* (spiced rice with meat) or *luqaimat* (sweet dumplings).

---

## **Day 2 – Culture & Museums**
- **Museum of Islamic Art (MIA)** – stunning architecture and Islamic art collections.
- Walk in **MIA Park** for scenic harbor views.
- Visit **National Museum of Qatar** – modern architecture and Qatar’s history.
- Evening: dinner or coffee at **The Pearl-Qatar**, a luxurious waterfront island.

---

## **Day 3 – Traditional Markets & Modern Downtown**
- Morning at **Souq Waqif** – shop for souvenirs, spices, traditional crafts.
- Explore **Msheireb Downtown Doha** – restored heritage area with a mix of tradition and modernity.
- Optional: **Katara Cultural Village** – galleries, theaters, and occasional cultural shows.

---

## **Day 4 – Beach Day & Relaxation**
- Relax at **Katara Beach** or a private beach at a resort.
- Lunch with a sea view.
- Optional sunset cruise or evening stroll along the Corniche.

---

## **Day 5 – Desert Adventure**
- Full-day **desert safari**:
  - 4x4 dune drive (*dune bashing* – gentle if desired).
  - Visit the **Inland Sea (Khor Al Adaid)** – unique desert-meets-sea landscape.
  - Optional sandboarding or photography.
- Evening BBQ dinner in the desert.

---

## **Day 6 – Leisure & Shopping**
- Morning: relax at hotel or enjoy a spa session.
- Visit **Villaggio Mall** or **Mall of Qatar** – souvenirs, cafes, shopping.
- Evening: walk along the Corniche or enjoy sunset views of the Doha skyline.

---

## **Day 7 – Departure**
- Relaxed breakfast at hotel.
- Optional quick visits:
  - **Falcon Souq** – see Qatar’s traditional falconry culture.
  - **Dhow Harbor** – traditional boats and maritime atmosphere.
- Transfer to the airport for departure.

---

## **Tips**
- **Transport:** Uber, taxi, and Doha Metro are convenient.
- Keep at least **one flexible day** for relaxing or unexpected discoveries.
- For photography: **sunset at the Corniche or Inland Sea** is stunning.
- Dress modestly in public areas; beaches and resorts allow casual attire.
