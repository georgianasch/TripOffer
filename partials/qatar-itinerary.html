Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival in Doha

Settle in & Evening Stroll

  • Check-in and settle at your hotel
  • Take a stroll along the **Corniche** – enjoy views of the modern skyline
  • Dinner at **Souq Waqif**: try local dishes like machboos (spiced rice with meat)
  • Sample luqaimat (sweet dumplings) for dessert

Day 2 — Culture & Museums

Museum of Islamic Art & National Museum

  • **Museum of Islamic Art (MIA)** – stunning architecture and Islamic art collections
  • Walk in **MIA Park** for scenic harbor views
  • Visit **National Museum of Qatar** – modern architecture and Qatar's history
  • Evening: dinner or coffee along the **Corniche** with waterfront views

Day 3 — Traditional Markets & Modern Downtown

Souq Waqif & Msheireb

  • Morning at **Souq Waqif** – shop for souvenirs, spices, traditional crafts
  • Explore **Msheireb Downtown Doha** – restored heritage area
  • Mix of tradition and modernity in architecture and design
  • Optional: **Katara Cultural Village** – galleries, theaters, cultural shows

Day 4 — Beach Day & Relaxation

Katara Beach & Seaside Dining

  • Relax at **Katara Beach** or a private beach at a resort
  • Lunch with a sea view
  • Optional sunset cruise along the coast
  • Evening stroll along the Corniche

Day 5 — Desert Adventure

Inland Sea & Desert Safari

  • Full-day **desert safari** with 4x4 dune driving (gentle if desired)
  • Visit the **Inland Sea (Khor Al Adaid)** – UNESCO-recognized natural reserve
  • Unique desert-meets-sea landscape
  • Optional sandboarding or photography
  • Evening BBQ dinner in the desert under the stars

Day 6 — Leisure & Shopping

Spa, Malls & Sunset Views

  • Morning: relax at hotel or enjoy a spa session
  • Visit **Villaggio Mall** or **Mall of Qatar** – souvenirs, cafes, shopping
  • Explore Italian-style architecture at Villaggio
  • Evening: walk along the Corniche or enjoy sunset views of the Doha skyline

Day 7 — Departure

Final Moments in Doha

  • Relaxed breakfast at hotel
  • Optional quick visits: **Falcon Souq** – see Qatar's traditional falconry culture
  • **Dhow Harbor** – traditional boats and maritime atmosphere
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping
  • Transfer to Hamad International Airport for departure
