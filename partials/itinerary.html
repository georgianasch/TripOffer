Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival in Shanghai

Pudong Airport → Hotel → The Bund

  • Arrive at Shanghai Pudong (PVG) or Hongqiao (SHA) airport
  • Transfer to hotel in Jing'an, People's Square, or French Concession area
  • Evening stroll along the Bund with illuminated Pudong skyline views
  • First taste of Shanghai street food

Day 2 — Shanghai Classic

Yu Garden → French Concession → Shanghai Tower

  • Yu Garden: traditional Chinese garden and Old City bazaar
  • French Concession: tree-lined streets, cafes, colonial architecture
  • Xintiandi: modern shopping and dining district
  • Shanghai Tower observation deck (632m) for panoramic city views

Day 3 — Day Trip to Suzhou

High-speed train: 25-30 min from Shanghai Hongqiao

  • Morning train to Suzhou, the "Venice of the East"
  • Humble Administrator's Garden: UNESCO World Heritage classical garden
  • Pingjiang Road: ancient canal street with traditional shops
  • Tiger Hill: iconic leaning pagoda and scenic park
  • Evening return to Shanghai

Day 4 — Day Trip to Hangzhou

High-speed train: 45-50 min from Shanghai Hongqiao

  • Morning train to Hangzhou, famous for West Lake
  • West Lake boat cruise: UNESCO site with pagodas and bridges
  • Longjing Tea Village: sample authentic Dragon Well green tea
  • Lingyin Temple: ancient Buddhist temple in bamboo forest
  • Evening return to Shanghai

Day 5 — Shanghai Relaxed

Temples → Museums → Shopping Streets

  • Jade Buddha Temple: serene Buddhist temple with jade Buddha statues
  • Shanghai Museum: world-class collection of Chinese art and artifacts
  • Jing'an Temple: impressive temple in the heart of modern Shanghai
  • Nanjing Road: pedestrian shopping street with neon lights

Day 6 — Optional Exploration

Zhujiajiao Water Town or Nanjing

  • Option A: Zhujiajiao ancient water town (Metro Line 17, ~1h)
  • Canal streets, stone bridges, traditional shops and restaurants
  • Option B: Day trip to Nanjing (high-speed train ~1h)
  • City walls, Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum, Confucius Temple area

Day 7 — Journey to Zhangjiajie

High-speed train: 6.5-7.5 hours

  • Morning departure from Shanghai to Zhangjiajie
  • Scenic train journey through Chinese countryside
  • Arrive late afternoon/evening
  • Check-in to hotel in Wulingyuan area (near park entrance)
  • Rest and prepare for mountain adventures

Day 8 — Zhangjiajie Avatar Mountains

National Forest Park — The Floating Mountains

  • Zhangjiajie National Forest Park: inspiration for Avatar's Hallelujah Mountains
  • Bailong Elevator: world's tallest outdoor lift (326m)
  • Yuanjiajie scenic area: towering quartzite sandstone pillars
  • Avatar Hallelujah Mountain (Southern Sky Column)
  • Tianzi Mountain: panoramic views of peak formations

Day 9 — Tianmen Mountain

Sky Walk & Heaven's Gate

  • World's longest cable car (7.5km, 30 min ride with incredible views)
  • Glass Skywalk: cliff-edge walkway 1,400m above ground
  • Heaven's Gate: massive natural arch in cliff face
  • 999 steps to the gate (or escalator option)
  • Cliff-hanging walkways and mountain paths

Day 10 — Glass Bridge (Optional)

Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon

  • Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Glass Bridge
  • World's longest (430m) and highest (300m) glass-bottomed bridge
  • Canyon hiking trails and waterfalls
  • Alternative: Relaxed day exploring Wulingyuan town
  • Pack and prepare for tomorrow's train to Beijing

Day 11 — Journey to Beijing

High-speed train: 6-6.5 hours

  • Morning/midday departure from Zhangjiajie to Beijing West
  • Cross China's heartland on comfortable G-train
  • Arrive late afternoon/evening in Beijing
  • Check-in to hotel in central location
  • Evening walk, first taste of Beijing cuisine

Day 12 — Beijing Imperial City

Tiananmen → Forbidden City → Jingshan Park

  • Tiananmen Square: world's largest public square
  • Forbidden City: massive imperial palace complex, UNESCO World Heritage
  • 180 acres, 980 buildings, former home of Chinese emperors
  • Jingshan Park: sunset view overlooking Forbidden City's golden roofs
  • Evening: Peking duck dinner

Day 13 — The Great Wall of China

Mutianyu Section — Cable Car & Toboggan

  • Day trip to Mutianyu section (less crowded than Badaling)
  • Cable car ride up to the Wall
  • Walk along restored watchtowers and battlements
  • Stunning mountain views and photo opportunities
  • Toboggan slide down (fun alternative to cable car descent)
  • Return to Beijing in late afternoon

Day 14 — Beijing Final & Departure

Summer Palace → Temple of Heaven → Flight Home

  • Summer Palace: imperial garden and lake, UNESCO World Heritage
  • Kunming Lake boat ride and Long Corridor walkway
  • Temple of Heaven: where emperors prayed for good harvests
  • Last-minute souvenir shopping
  • Transfer to Beijing airport for flight to Amsterdam
